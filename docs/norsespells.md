# NORSE MYTHOLOGY SPELLS AND EFFECTS

## 1. Galdr (Incantations and Vocal Spells)
Galdr was a vocal magic system utilizing specific poetic meters. The Norse believed physical sound could alter the fabric of reality.

### The Spells of Odin
* **The First Spell (Help)**: Relieves sickness, sorrow, and mental anguish.
* **The Fourth Spell (Fetters)**: Breaks physical chains and leg-shackles if captured by enemies.
* **The Fifth Spell (Arrow-Catching)**: Stops a flying battlefield arrow mid-air through sight alone.
* **The Eighth Spell (Peace-Weaving)**: Quenches hatred and brings immediate reconciliation between enemies.
* **The Eleventh Spell (Shield-Song)**: Sung into a shield before warriors go to battle to ensure they return unharmed.
* **The Twelfth Spell (Necromancy)**: Forces a hanged corpse to walk and talk to share secrets.

## 2. Runic Spells (Words of Power)
Runes were cosmic blueprints. Carving, coloring with blood, and naming them generated concrete magical consequences.

### Key Formulaic Spells
* **Alu**: Found on ancient amulets. Its effect was to ward off evil spirits and keep curses away from the wearer.
* **Lina Laukar**: Translates to "flax and onion." It was a spell for fertility, growth, and preserving health.
* **Tiwaz Triplication (ttt)**: Stacking the Tiwaz rune three times. Its effect was to grant victory and luck in physical combat.

## 3. Seiðr (Shamanic Fate-Weaving)
Seiðr required entering a deep trance state to manipulate the web of fate. It was traditionally associated with the goddess Freyja.

### Ritual Applications
* **Spá (Prophetic Seership)**: Practiced by a Völva. Its effect allowed the user to peer directly into the future.
* **Útiseta (Sitting-Out)**: A spell of sitting outside at night on burial mounds. The effect was communing with spirits or the dead for hidden knowledge.
* **Mind-Weaving (Mental Manipulation)**: Woven into an enemy's mind. The effect induced sudden panic, forgetfulness, or a freezing mental fog in battle.
