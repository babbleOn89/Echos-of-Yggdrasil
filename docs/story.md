# ECHOS OF YGGDRASIL

# STORY NOTES (ROUGH DRAFT)

# Core Themes

* Echoes of Yggdrasil
A descendant of Odin uncovers forbidden Norse magic while a conflicted angel manipulates events from the shadows as Heaven attempts to prevent another Ragnarok.

* Curiosity vs Authority
* Faith vs Morality
* Knowledge vs Fear
* Cycles of Destruction
* Questioning Beliefs
* Breaking Away From Expected Roles

---

# Protagonist

* Descendant of Odin
* Lives in Minnesota
* Discovers traces of Norse magic
* Curiosity drives the plot
* Not attempting to start Ragnarok
* Wants answers and understanding

---

# Angel Character

* Extremely powerful NPC
* Appears periodically to assist the player
* Initially works for Heaven
* Sympathizes with protagonist
* Turns against Heaven
* Mirrors the protagonist's journey of questioning authority

Possible:

* Playable during certain story sections
* Bullet Hell gameplay during angel chapters

---

# Christian God

* Not intended to be evil
* Defeated other pantheons long ago
* Believed their actions threatened more than their own regions
* Feared world-ending events
* Broke pathways between realms
* Suppressed dangerous divine powers
* Fears the return of Norse magic
* Views protagonist as a potential catalyst for Ragnarok

---

# Bifrost

* Destroyed/shattered after divine conflicts
* Travel between realms became nearly impossible
* Major story goal involves repairing it
* Fragments may be scattered across multiple realms

---

# Yggdrasil

* Central world structure
* Connects realms
* Ancient pathways may still exist inside it
* Possible method of travel after Bifrost destruction
* Could contain forgotten locations and lost knowledge

# Act Structure (Very Rough)

## Act 1

* Minnesota
* Discover Norse magic
* Introduce angel
* Introduce Heaven conflict

## Act 2

* Search for Bifrost fragments
* Learn history of divine conflict
* Explore hidden pathways

## Act 3

* Reach Asgard
* Learn truth about old gods
* Gather remaining fragments

## Act 4

* Heaven
* Angel storyline
* Open rebellion against Heaven

## Act 5

* Yggdrasil
* Final revelations -- Angel reveals Ragnarok was the goal
* Resolution of conflict

# Random Ideas

* Norse magic feels forbidden/forgotten
* Ancient runes function as magic system
* Heaven has been rewriting history
* The protagonist is not "chosen"; he simply discovered something hidden
* The angel becomes increasingly conflicted throughout the story
* Repairing the Bifrost serves as both gameplay progression and story progression
*The Christian God is haunted by echoes of the last Ragnarok.
* The angel is obsessed with restoring echoes of the old world.
* Everybody is listening to echoes.
* Nobody knows the full truth.
